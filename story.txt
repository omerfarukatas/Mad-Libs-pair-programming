Star Wars is a captivating[a] story[n] of good[a] versus evil in a galaxy far far away. There are fierce[a] battles between huge[a] ships[n] in outer[a] space and deadly[a] duels with omer[n] called light[a] sabers. Robots[n] called droids are helpers and companions[a] to the heroes. A dark power called The Sith[n] forces[v] people to do bad[a] things, like killing[v] innocents. The Jedi use The Force for the light[a] side and the Sith wield[v] it for the dark[a] side.
